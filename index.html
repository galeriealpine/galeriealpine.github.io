We are a group of creative people who got together with the vision to create an exhibition space for our work and set up an art centre for Villars-sur-Ollon, in the heart of the Vaud Alps. Here you can find out more information about the gallery, our artists, the association, the latest news, and where to find us. You will also find us on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.
